My aunt taught me how to do pottery when I was eleven years old, and I've been hooked ever since! I love making things with my hands, and am always striving to learn new techniques and better my art through practice and observation.

If you are interested in a custom order, understand that it will take 4 to 6 weeks to complete. I throw the pieces on the wheel, then they have to dry, get fired, then glazed, then fired again! Although it may take some time, each piece is hand made with love and passion, and you will know it when you hold it in your hands.

Thanks so much for checking out my shop. Message me with any questions or if you just want to say hi!
